Token cost: 8,676 vs 27,684 tokens per run. Over 3x difference.

Consistency: One framework varied by just 0.2 points across all runs. Another swung by 1.4 points.

Statistical tests confirmed it. Quality differences between most frameworks? Indistinguishable from noise. Latency differences? p = 0.000001. Extremely real.

The takeaway: quality is table stakes now. The framework isn't the intelligence layer — the model is. What actually matters is how efficiently the framework gets out of the way.
